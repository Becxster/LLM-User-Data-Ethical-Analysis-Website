Digital ethics case study

Emerging Risks from AI Chatbots: Data Privacy in User Interactions

This site presents an in-depth case study on the user privacy issues surrounding Large Language Models (LLMs), outlining the core facts, analyzing the ethical dilemmas, and exploring broader societal impacts and possible solutions.

At a glance

Topic
Collection of "private" user-chatbot conversations
Core dilemma
Many of the largest LLM companies utilize user data to inform their own models, thereby increasing their own market value. Many users are not aware this chat data is being sold, raising privacy concerns.
Main stakeholders
AI Users, AI Companies, State & Federal Regulators
Focus
Description, ethical analysis, societal impact.
